End Hunger & Malnutrition

Chronic malnutrition threatens the health and development of a third of the world’s population — especially pregnant women and children. When you donate to effective hunger charities, you’re helping the world survive and thrive.

Chronic malnutrition comes from deficiencies in iodine, folic acid, vitamin A, and other micronutrients that people in affluent countries typically consume in their normal diets.

Iodine deficiency is the leading preventable cause of intellectual and developmental disabilities in the world. A lack of folic acid can cause anemia, and low vitamin A is the leading cause of preventable blindness in children. Poor nutrition in the first thousand days of a child’s life can stunt their growth — a condition associated with impared cognitive ability, and reduced school and work performance. Nearly half of all under-five deaths in the world are due to malnutrition. These devastating consequences are all preventable with simple, cost-effective interventions like food fortification.
